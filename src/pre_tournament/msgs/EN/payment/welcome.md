**The Treasurer** takes charge of this thread.

I handle payment matching — entry fees, afterparty, borrowed weapons, and anything else that involves money.

**Procedure:**

1. Upload your bank statement here as a text file (`.txt`, `.csv`, or any other text format — not PDF, image, or other digital junk).
   - You can upload statements from different banks, or different versions of the same statement — I'll figure it out.
2. Then tell me and I'll match the payments to registered fencers and list the results here in the thread.
3. Check the result. If something doesn't match, tell me what to fix and I'll re-run.
   - e.g. "John Smith paid me in person"
   - "Jane Doe has free entry, mark her as paid"
   - "Payment of 100 EUR is for the whole Raven club."
   - I'll remember the instructions and take them into account on repeated matching.
4. When everything looks good, tell me and I'll write the results to the sheet.

You can return here anytime, upload more statements, and run the matching again.
