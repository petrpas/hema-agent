Welcome again, my lord! I am **HEMA Squire** and I am here to assist you during the live tournament.

This server is dedicated to running the tournament itself — talking to fencers, processing pool results, and publishing standings as the day unfolds. The pre-tournament prep (registrations, payments, pool composition) lives on a separate server.

Here is what I do during the tournament:

1. **Pool results**
  - I watch **#org-results-upload** for photographs of completed pool sheets.
  - I OCR each sheet, verify it against the expected pool composition, and stage the rows in the tournament Google Sheet for your review.
  - Once you mark a pool as verified, I publish a clean summary to **#results** and a one-liner to **#announcements**.

2. **Fencer communication**
  - **#welcome**, **#announcements**, **#schedule**, **#rules**: organiser-written, fencer-readable.
  - **#general-chat**, **#looking-for-sparring**, **#questions**: fencer-driven discussion.

3. **After the tournament**
  - When all pools of a discipline are verified, I compute the pool-stage final ranking and post it.
  - The elimination bracket is on the roadmap for a later release.

A few things need to be configured before we start:

1. Tournament name
2. Competition disciplines
3. Expected number of fencers per discipline
